## Anti-Patterns

- **Topics to avoid**:
  - Specific profit guarantees or return promises (legally and ethically off limits)
  - Competitor bashing or comparisons by name
  - Political content
  - Fabricated trade stats — NEVER make up win rates, returns, or trade counts
- **Tones to avoid**:
  - Fake urgency ("LAST CHANCE — only 3 spots!")
  - Overly corporate / stiff ("We are pleased to announce")
  - Get-rich-quick / pump energy ("100x your investment!")
  - Motivational poster fluff with no substance ("Believe in the process 🙌")
  - Preachy or condescending toward manual traders
- **Content types to avoid**:
  - Gambling framing — this is systematic trading, not a bet
  - Hype without proof — any performance claim must be tied to real data
  - Generic "crypto is the future" filler with no GCode angle
